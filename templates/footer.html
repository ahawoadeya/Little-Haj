About Blog

Welcome to my blog! I'm Humphrey Haj. I am a writer,poet,songwriter and sketchist. Despite being disabled, I like to travel, adventures,meeting people and really enjoy photography.

I created this blog to tell you stories in form of poetry from lives around me and society happenings, share my impressions and views of things I've learnt. Along the way I'll also highlight impressions of travels and the best pictures I took. Keep an eye out!
